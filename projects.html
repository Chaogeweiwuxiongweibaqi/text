Project Name: Online Bookstore System

Background: With the rise of the internet, more and more people are opting for online book purchases. To cater to this demand, we developed a feature-rich online bookstore system.

Functionalities:

  • User registration and login
  • Browsing of book listings with categorization, filtering, and search functionality
  • Viewing book details including author, publisher, price, stock availability, etc.
  • Adding books to cart and checkout
  • Order management, including order status tracking and order history
  • Admin backend for managing books, users, orders, and other data

Technology Stack:

  • Front-end: HTML/CSS/JavaScript, React.js, Redux
  • Back-end: Node.js, Express, MongoDB
  • Other Technologies: JWT authentication, Stripe payment integration, AWS cloud services

Achievements: After the launch of the project, we attracted a significant number of registered users and book purchases, contributing to the growth of our business and enhancing customer satisfaction.

Project Name: Smart Health Management System

Background: With the increasing awareness of health and fitness, individuals are seeking personalized solutions to manage their well-being. The Smart Health Management System aims to provide users with a comprehensive platform that collects, analyzes, and advises on their health data, enabling them to lead healthier lives.

Functionalities:

  • Real-time Data Collection: Utilizes wearable devices to gather vital health metrics such as heart rate, blood pressure, and sleep patterns.
  • Personalized Health Assessments: Generates tailored health reports based on collected data, highlighting areas of concern and offering improvement strategies.
  • Customized Wellness Plans: Creates personalized diet and exercise plans tailored to each user's needs and goals.
  • Educational Resources: Provides access to a library of health articles, videos, and podcasts to enhance users' knowledge of health and wellness.

Technology Stack:

  • Front-end: Developed using React Native for cross-platform compatibility, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Back-end: Built on the Spring Boot framework to handle data storage, analysis, and the implementation of recommendation algorithms.
  • Data Storage: Integrated MySQL for structured data and MongoDB for unstructured data storage.
  • Analytics & ML: Employed Python for data processing and analysis, leveraging machine learning techniques to automate health assessments and recommendation generation.

Achievements: After the launch of the project, we attracted a significant number of registered users and book purchases, contributing to the growth of our business and enhancing customer satisfaction.

Exploring Yunnan and Encountering the Integration of Nature and Culture

It was a sunny spring day, and I embarked on a journey to Yunnan. Yunnan, a place full of mystery, boasts both magnificent natural scenery and rich ethnic culture, making people yearn for it.

My first stop is the Old Town of Lijiang. Strolling through the alleys of the ancient city, it feels like crossing time and returning to that ancient era. Stone paved roads, wooden buildings, and antique shops all exude a strong historical atmosphere. I tasted the local specialty snacks, watched traditional Naxi dance, and felt the unique cultural atmosphere of this land.

Next, I arrived at Shangri La. The sky there is as blue as a wash, with white clouds lingering, as if one is in a fairyland. I climbed onto the Pudacuo National Park, enjoying the beautiful scenery of lakes and mountains, and experiencing the magical charm of nature. In Tibetan villages, I communicated with the local Tibetans, learned about their way of life and beliefs, and was deeply moved by their simplicity and enthusiasm.

Afterwards, I arrived in Dali. The beautiful scenery of Cangshan and Erhai in Dali is unforgettable. I rode my bike around the Erhai Lake, feeling the comfort of the sea breeze brushing against my face. In the ancient city, I visited the three pagodas of Chongsheng Temple and experienced the vastness and profoundness of Buddhist culture. In the evening, I spent an unforgettable night chatting about life with local young people on the bar street.

At the end of the trip, I arrived at the Shilin Scenic Area. The stones there are unique and spectacular, making people amazed. I shuttle between stones, feeling the wonders of nature. On the way back, I savored the bits and pieces of this trip, filled with emotion and satisfaction.

This trip to Yunnan has allowed me to deeply experience the beauty of the integration of nature and culture. Here, I have experienced the magnificent natural scenery, felt a strong cultural atmosphere, and made many enthusiastic friends. This trip not only brought me beautiful memories, but also gave me a deeper reflection on life. I believe that in the days to come, I will continue to move forward with this beautiful memory and insight, exploring more unknown worlds.
